Bio


Hello, I am Arya Varaste, a last-year Information Technology master's student at the University of Tehran.

My master's thesis focuses on Depression and Anxiety Detection through Learners' Behavior Analysis in electronic learning systems. By analyzing learners' behavior, I aim to contribute to the development of effective strategies for identifying and addressing mental health challenges in educational settings. Throughout my master's program, I have authored two papers.

Before pursuing my master's, I completed my bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at Amirkabir University of Technology in Tehran. During this time, I undertook a captivating project focused on the Design of a Dentigerous Lesion Detection System from Radiographic Images using Deep Learning methods. By employing state-of-the-art techniques in deep learning, I aimed to enhance the detection and diagnosis of dental conditions, ultimately contributing to improved oral healthcare outcomes.

My research interests span various areas within the field of Information Technology, including Data Mining, Data Analytics and Visualization, Machine Learning, and Trustworthy-AI. I believe that these cutting-edge areas hold immense potential to revolutionize industries such as healthcare, human-computer interaction (HCI), and e-Learning.

As a driven and curious individual, I continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and contribute to the advancement of Information Technology. I take great pride in my accomplishments thus far, and I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead.
